Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my web development services

How long does it take to build a website?
Typically 1-2 weeks for a basic business website, and 2-3 weeks for e-commerce sites. The timeline depends on the complexity and your responsiveness.
What do I need to provide?
You'll need: 1) Business information (logo, description, contact details), 2) Content (text and images), 3) Domain name (I can help you register one), 4) Hosting (included in some packages).
Do you offer maintenance after launch?
Yes! All packages include 1 month of free support. After that, maintenance plans start from ₦5,000/month for updates, backups, and security monitoring.
Can I update the website myself?
Absolutely! I provide training on how to update content. For e-commerce sites, I build admin panels where you can manage products, orders, and customers easily.
